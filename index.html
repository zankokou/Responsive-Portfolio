About Me

Hi there! My name is Kenneth Huynh. You can call me Ken. I am currently a Lv1 Coder on the journey to be a Master Web Developer. So far, I have Lv1 Mastery of HTML and a Lv1 Mastery of CSS. This webpage took me over 10 hours alone! That is quite a long time! Before going on this journey I went to school at the University of Illinois at Chicago. I studied Finance and worked in the Insurance Industry. It was a cool gig but it did not fill my need to be creative with my imagination.
Growing up I was full of ideas, whether it was thinking of games to play, writing short stories, or playing with Legos. I have left that part of myself in the shadows for a while.. BUT NO MORE! Learning to code is my outlet to use my creativity and do something that I am passionate about. Oh and look at that! Just leveled up to Lv2 Coder! Woo!


Thanks for reading!
